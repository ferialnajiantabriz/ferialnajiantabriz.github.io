Bio & Experience

I am Ferial Najiantabriz, a dedicated computer scientist and data scientist with a strong background in machine learning, natural language processing, and full-stack development. I recently earned my Master’s degree in Computer Science from the University of Oklahoma, where I engaged in a variety of impactful research and applied projects. My work has included developing machine learning pipelines for healthcare prediction, designing and deploying interactive dashboards using Streamlit and R Shiny, and contributing to robust, end-to-end data analysis systems. In addition to my research, I served as a Teaching Assistant for courses such as Algorithm Analysis and Computer Security, where I provided academic support and technical mentorship to students.

My technical skill set spans Python, SQL, and cloud services like Microsoft Azure, with practical experience in tools such as TensorFlow, PyTorch, Docker, and GitHub Actions. I have authored peer-reviewed research in clustering-based recommender systems and continue to explore topics in deep learning and language models. I am particularly passionate about building intelligent systems that are both technically sound and user-focused. My goal is to contribute to teams and organizations that prioritize innovation, collaborative learning, and data-driven decision-making.
